Questions

Before you decide.

Do I need a credit card to start? add

No. You can track your first 5 trips with no account and no card at all — download, drive, and see the deductions add up. Create an account only when you're ready to lock the savings in.

What counts toward the Free plan's limits? add

The Free plan covers 5 auto-tracked trips and 5 receipts per calendar month, for one user. It's built for trying ExpenseFlow and for demos — upgrade to Pro for unlimited tracking and export.

How does annual billing work? add

Pay yearly and you get roughly two months free — about 17% off. Pro works out to $70/year and Business to $100/user/year. Toggle "Annual" above to see the per-month equivalent.

What's the difference between Pro and Business? add

Pro is for individuals — unlimited tracking, export and the full web app. Business adds everything a finance team needs: a shared dashboard, approval workflows, AI budget alerts and the accountant portal, for 5–50 seats.

I'm an accountant. Is there a partner program? add

Yes. Clients you refer get 3 months free, and you get a view-only portal to review their logs, waypoint accuracy and data integrity. Standardized exports mean the same clean format from every client.

Can I change or cancel my plan anytime? add

Anytime. Upgrade, downgrade or cancel from your account settings — changes take effect at your next billing cycle, with no lock-in.
