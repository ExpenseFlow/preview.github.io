Every drive you don't write down is a deduction you can't claim. Manual logs miss 20–30% of trips. Automatic tracking catches all of them.

Apps that snap to the nearest tower lose 3–5% of distance on winding roads. ExpenseFlow logs real waypoints to ±1 metre.
