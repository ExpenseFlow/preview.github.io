Features

Both halves of the job.

Mileage and receipts, auto-tracked and joined into one audit-ready record. Here's everything under the hood — from the GPS pipeline to the accountant portal.

app.expenseflow.app/trips

01 — Tracking

Automatic GPS trip tracking.

Activity recognition starts the moment you move — no manual "start trip," no forgotten drives. The MARUTI burst-sampling algorithm fires 3-point GPS bursts on motion, turns and heartbeat, then averages them for accuracy without draining your battery.

  • check Background tracking that starts itself
  • check Persistent, honest notification — if you see it, you're earning
  • check Real-time tax-savings ticker as you drive
app.expenseflow.app/receipts

02 — Capture

In-the-moment receipt capture.

Snap the receipt at the pump or counter and Gemini-powered OCR reads the vendor, amount and date. ExpenseFlow pairs it to the right trip automatically, so the paper never gets a chance to vanish into a glovebox.

  • check AI receipt OCR — vendor, amount, date
  • check Auto-paired to the matching trip
  • check Trip + receipt become one CRA-compliant record
app.expenseflow.app/dashboard

03 — Organize

Desk-to-Dash workflow.

Capture on mobile, organize on desktop. The full web app is your editing surface: bulk-classify trips as Work or Personal, accept AI smart-context hints that remember why you drove somewhere, and export a clean log structured by year and jurisdiction.

  • check Bulk Work / Personal categorization
  • check AI hints: "Was this a Project Materials trip for Client X?"
  • check Year-over-year, quarter and month analytics

The competitive moat

Accuracy you can defend in an audit.

Cell-tower snapping loses 3–5% of distance on winding roads — and that's deduction gone. ExpenseFlow runs a 5-stage server-side pipeline that turns raw GPS into verifiable waypoints.
